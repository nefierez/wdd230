Know About Guatemala

Guatemala, Country of Eternal Spring

The name "Guatemala" means "place of many trees."

Guatemala's abundance of biologically significant and unique ecosystems includes many endemic species and contributes to Mesoamerica's designation as a biodiversity hotspot.

Demographic Trends

Guatemala has an estimated population of around 17.2 million.

This statistic shows the age structure in Guatemala as of 2019. In 2019, about 33.2% percent of Guatemala's total population were aged 0 to 15 years old, while only 0.5% percent of the total population was 85 years old or more.

People

Guatemala is a multicultural society, though most Guatemalans have varying degrees of European (predominantly Spaniards) and Amerindian ancestry.

Guatemalans are also colloquially nicknamed Chapines in other Spanish-speaking countries of Latin America.

Traditions

Guatemala is absolutely rich with cultural celebrations; festivals burst with outrageous color, ancient traditions, and rampant symbolism.
